BeanBoard Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-09-21

BeanBoard (“we”) provides a collaboration app for deliveries, tasks, and alerts.

Data We Collect

  • Account details you provide
  • Photos/videos you choose to capture or upload (via camera or library)
  • Device identifiers and diagnostics for notifications and reliability

Camera and Photos

Camera access is used only when you capture media. Media you select is uploaded per your action. We do not access your media without your choice.

Location

We do not collect precise location. Platform prompts may appear due to APIs or SDKs used for notifications.

Use of Data

To provide features (attach media to deliveries, tasks, and alerts), secure access, support, and improve the service.

Sharing

With service providers (e.g., hosting, notifications). We do not sell personal data.

Retention

Kept as needed to provide the service or as required by law. You may request deletion where applicable.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards; no method is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may access, correct, or delete your data, or object to processing.
